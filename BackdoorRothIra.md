# Backdoor Roth IRA

## Accounts

- **Eric** — Fidelity
- **Mia** — Fidelity

## Funding

- **Eric's Traditional IRA** — fund directly.
- **Mia's Traditional IRA** — fund Eric's individual account first, then transfer to Mia's Traditional IRA.

## Conversion

Convert the full balance. After conversion, Fidelity may still deposit dividends into the Traditional IRA, requiring a second conversion — so don't close the Traditional IRA manually after converting.

## Reopening a Closed Account

Traditional IRA accounts with no balance will auto-close. To reopen, log in to Fidelity on desktop web — does not appear to work on mobile app or mobile web.
